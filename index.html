+ About Us

East Dragon Karate Academy is a dojo in Dover, NJ,
located at 58 Bassett Hwy. The sensei is Sensei Bill Roberts.
We teach many martial arts, focusing chiefly on Tai Chi and Isshinryu Karate.
It's a wonderful place to be.
